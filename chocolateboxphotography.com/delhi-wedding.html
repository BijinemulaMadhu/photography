We were in Delhi on a cold winter morning one fine day with our team, excited to shoot a fun wedding filled with moments and memories. Cameras all charged and energies all high, we start off to shoot Arpita and Mohits wedding events with a great start. To give you a small introduction, Arpita and Mohit were a match made not in heaven, but at another wedding, totally desi style dont you think? They met and chatted up at their friends wedding and that was the beginning of a journey forever. Were here to shoot their wedding and the rest as they say is history. Arpita is a bride with a unique sense of style. Strong and graceful, charming and charismatic at the same time. Minimalist at heart and also as a bride, Arpita got a minimalistic Mehendi design for her big day unlike any other bride. She chose to spend more time having fun with her near and dear ones at the event. Her blue hair streaks are an absolute stunner that complimented her look and the personality. The families of both Arpita and Mohit are warm, fun loving and very compassionate which was an element of support for us. Arpita and Mohit are people who share their passion for food,books and movies. Be it a regional kannada movie or an intense Christopher Nolan one, they watch together and enjoy it the same way and they also share their passion for cooking. Their wedding ceremony is was filled with fun, frolic and lots of color. Their sangeet happened to fall exactly at the time of Halloween which made it an ideal instance of have a themed party where the kids had a ball of a time. Arpita and Mohit tied the knot on one evening after the baraat arrived in all its grandness. They took the seven pheras, vowing to be there for one another, always and forever.
