“Shivani & Parikshit, a match made in heaven - literally."

Shivani and Parikshit, a match made in heaven - literally. Youll discover why we say this very soon. Chocolate box Photography had the amazing opportunity to cover this grand wedding of two pilots of Jet airways. Match made in heaven isnt it? The wedding ceremonies unfolded in a sequential manner where we witnessed great planning and execution to such perfection. Shivani was a bride totally comfortable of her style and the outfits she chose for the wedding were flawless. Parikshit on the other hand is your ideal tall,dark and handsome man with a great charm to him. The Mehendi event was colorful, fun filled with lots of friends and family. Mehendi artist Veena Nagda’s designs were so magical and pretty. Shivani’s pet dog was a part of the ceremony too which took the cuteness quotient up by a notch. For their Sangeet, the bride wore an Outfit by Ridhi mehra. The highlight of the night was performance by the bride and her sister. Their dance set the stage on fire and also the set the mood for the evening. The candid photos shot at the events will speak for themselves. The wedding ceremony was held at Grand Hyatt in Mumbai. Shivani, the bride adorned a traditional Kanchivaram saree and Parikshit wore a sherwani in pastel shade. The couple looked royal and so perfect for the event. The venue decor added to the aesthetic beauty, it was the best a photographer could ask for. Shivani and Parikshit are both pilots who share their interest in music. They met for the first time on a flight to Kolkata and it was serendipity. Their sync in music got them closer which eventually led them to take their relationship to the next level.
